## Why "Rainbow Crow"?

There's a story that circulates online called *Rainbow Crow* — usually presented
as a Lenape tale about a crow with rainbow feathers who flies to the sun to bring
fire back to the freezing animals, scorching its feathers black and its voice
rough in the act of generosity.

Here's the thing: **the Lenape people don't claim that story.** It's widely
misattributed — a story *about* provenance that doesn't have honest provenance of
its own. We named the shop after it deliberately, and we take that lesson to
heart. Where things come from matters. Who gets credited matters. We try to know
the provenance of what we stock and recommend, and to be honest when we don't.

And the rest of the name is simpler:

**Rainbow**, because this is a queer space. Not queer-tolerant — queer, on
purpose, from the foundation.

**Crow**, because the owner loves them, and because crows are everything we
aspire to be: smart, imaginative, playful, language-oriented, loyal to their
flock, and entirely unbothered by anyone's opinion of them.

## What this place is

A books and games shop, yes — RPGs, board games, card games, books, zines,
tarot, and accessories. But the shop is the anchor, not the point. The point is
a **community**: game nights, a Discord, a place to find your table and your
people.

Come say hi on the [community page](/community), or browse [the catalog](/catalog).

## Get in touch

We're a small team, so this is easy: reach out on the [community page](/community) or find us in person at a game night. If you're looking for something specific for the shop, tell us the title — we're always happy to track things down.
