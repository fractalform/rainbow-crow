## Rainbow Crow Online!

This is my first post on the new website. It's been exciting learning about full stack websites and the potential for curating a catalog here. I've started building a catalog of books, games, and accessories that I want to see all together in a space some day, but for now it's great to see them all here in one place. 

I'm working on building a few new features here, including an email subscription, events management, and potential for discord integration. As I'm adding those elements into the website, I'm also looking forward to launching our first pop-up event, coming soon!

Additionally, the artwork cards I have on the site right now are just placeholders; I'm still designing and organizing the branding of the site. Hopefully I'll settle on that design soon, not only for the site but for creating some merch that we can add into the catalog. I'd really enjoy having some merch to share with the community from the start of what I'm building. 

As far as first posts go, this is mostly an update of how many things are left to do on the site. But, it's a great start, and it's pretty inspirational for me to see the catalog. More to come soon!
